ESP32 projects

Experimental projects to learn more about embedded software development. I made many different small projects making use of ESP32 all features.

Making use of ESP32 and different sensors extending it's capabilities. I implemented the microprocessor to display messages me and my friend sent to each other using MQTT. With a push of a button I was able to send a message on his topic and he on my topic. Telling the other person that it was time for a gaming session.


Another project included the christmas tree. Using a ultra sonic sender and reciever I made a measuring device which was able to measure the water level in the foot of the christmas tree. If the water level was too low, it sent a notification to my smart home which sent a notification to my phone. Leting me know I need to fill it with water.


Other features used were: Bluetooth, Wifi, Deep sleep, Ultra Sonic sensors, lights, I2C displays, dimmers, buzzers and probably more.
